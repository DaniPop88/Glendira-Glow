Our Story
Founded by Glenda Perez Ariola, Glendira Glow Collective began as a small community shop in Lobo, Batangas.
Inspired by Filipino resilience and creativity, we built our brand on quality apparel, fair pricing, and customer care.
Our Mission
To provide stylish, affordable apparel for every Filipino.
To support local suppliers and promote sustainable clothing practices.
To create a brand rooted in Filipino culture and hospitality.
Our Values
Honesty: We stand behind every product.
Service: Customers are our priority (“Mahal kayo ng Glendira!”).
Innovation: Always updated with trends to keep you glowing.
