Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 28, 2025
At Glendira Glow Collective, we value your trust and are dedicated to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information whenever you shop with us, visit our website, or interact with our team.
1. Information We Collect
We collect only the information necessary to provide you with the best possible service. This includes:
  • Your name and contact details (email, phone number, delivery address)
  • Order and payment information
  • Communication records (such as messages, feedback, or inquiries)
  • Website usage data to help us improve your shopping experience
2. How We Use Your Information
We use your information to:

  • Contact you for order updates or clarifications

Rest assured, we do not sell or rent your information to third parties.
3. How We Protect Your Data
We implement industry-standard security measures to keep your information safe. Access to your data is limited to authorized personnel only, and we regularly review our procedures to ensure your privacy is maintained.
4. Sharing and Disclosure
We may share your information only in the following situations:

  • If required by law, regulation, or legal process
5. Your Choices and Rights
You have the right to:
  • Review, update, or correct your personal information at any time
  • Request deletion of your information (if not required for transactions or legal obligations)
  • Opt out of promotional emails or messages
To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@glendiracollective.online.
6. Updates to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through our website or directly to you if you have an active order or account.
